French Crepes

Picture of Finished Dish

Description

A couple paragraphs describing french crepes

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Butter
  • Vanilla Exctract

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients together in a large bowl.
  2. Refrigerate batter for 30 mins (Good in fridge for one day)
  3. Remove batter from fridge and heat a large skillet over medium-heat.
  4. Melt 1 tbsp of butter on large skillet, adding 1/4 cup of crepe batter.
  5. Remove the crepe when its done, about 2-3 minutes for each crepe.
